SISU Technologies

I currently work for SISU Technologies. I have designed our website, many apps for our clients and one app for SISU.

I designed our website and coded it (HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP). It is responsive and compatible with every major browser. You can see the website, with more information on SISU Technologies, our services and a few of our projects at sisu.mx

This app is SISU App Installer, which we use to let our clients test our applications while they are in the development process. I designed it and coded the HTML, CSS and some JavaScript. Compatible with iOS and Android.

Data Gathering

“Con Participación” used manual methods to gather information on their new affiliates all around the country. We created a mobile app that automates the process and allows easy storage on the central affiliate database.
I designed the app and coded the HTML and CSS. Works on Android. Developed by SISU Technologies.

TSYS México

TSYS already had a database with critical information on the business. We collaborated developing a system’s front-end that displays it to key users. This system allows for a much faster access to critical information compared with the report generations and manual data processing.
I worked on the implementation of the web version, and redesigned it, although this redesign has not been developed yet. App works on iOS, Android and all major browsers. Developed by SISU Technologies.

ABC Leasing

For ABC Leasing, we developed three applications.

  1. The first was an app that made immediate car lease quotations.

  3. An administrator for the dashboards app was also developed as a web application.

I designed the dashboards app, administrator and coded the HTML, CSS and some JavaScript for these two and the web version of the quotations app.
